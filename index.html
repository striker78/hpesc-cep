Sharing Results of the R&D 2021 Security Hackathon

 

The Hackathon is about to start. Looking ahead, we will want to improve security for R&D as a whole. To achieve this goal, your Hackathon findings are a key accelerant, important to share with others in R&D so that we all can learn and improve.

 

Here is our plan so that you can share results:

7 minutes – each Hackathon Team speaker talks to their findings.

3 teams – groups of 3 teams per 30 minute recorded session.

2 sessions per day – Monday, April 5. to Friday, April 9. 2021 (total 10 sessions, 30 teams)

 

Hackathon Team speaker:

·        Be prepared to present your findings using this Powerpoint Template.

·        The security team will assign you to a session.
(Note: Any member of the Hackathon team can be the speaker)

 

All:

·        You will receive invites to the Hackathon Results sessions.
All sessions will be recorded and posted here.

·        On April 1: You will receive an invite to a super short survey to share your personal feedback about this PI25 Hackathon.
(Note: The survey is only open from April 1-11, 2021)
